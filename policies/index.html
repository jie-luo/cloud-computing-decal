Policies

Communication

The primary mode of communication will be through the course Piazza. If you need to contact course staff privately, you may contact us through the course email (not set up yet).

Enrollment

This course is a 2 unit DeCal. In order to enroll, students must attend the first lecture. We will then send enrollment codes to everyone. We offer 40 seats, but this may increase if we appoint more course staff.

Although this course has no prerequisites, we recommend having experience in at least one programming language.

Lecture

Due to the current COVID-19 pandemic, instruction will be online and webcasted or otherwise recorded.

There are 12 weeks of instruction with two 1-hour online lectures per week with discussions and labs dispersed throughout lecture.

Assignments

There will be weekly quizes and biweekly project assignments.

Grading

Overall grades will be determined from:

  • Project: 50%
  • Quizes: 30%
  • Attendance: 10%
  • Final reflection: 10%

Since this course is a DeCal, you must take it P/NP. Students with an overall percentage of 70% or greater will receive a P and students with an overall percentage of less than 70% will receive an NP.
